Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform inception, planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, supported by Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Cillian Murphy, delivering powerful performances.
